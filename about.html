About Me

I'm Mehreen — a frontend web developer, blogger, and freelance writer from Pakistan. I love combining clean, modern design with writing that’s useful, honest, and rooted in real-life experiences.

Declutter Queen is where I share smart organization tips, minimalist habits, and product reviews that help people create space and calm in their lives. Everything I post is simple, doable, and made for real homes and routines.

I also write essays on Medium covering health, sleep, minimalism, 2-hour daily routines, eco-conscious living, and screen breaks — including the popular piece, "No One Can Save You."

As a freelancer on Fiverr and Freelancer.com, I help brands and individuals with writing and web design — from landing pages to lifestyle content. I blend functionality with purpose, whether it's a piece of code or a piece of writing.

If you're looking for thoughtful content, clean design, or a creative collaborator, I’d love to connect.
